# PMC App

PMC App is a comprehensive ABA (Applied Behavior Analysis) therapy platform designed to support individuals with autism spectrum disorder and their caregivers. The application leverages digital technology to provide personalized therapy interventions and track behavioral progress.

**Key Features:**
*   **Interactive ABA Exercises:** Engaging, playful exercises designed to teach specific skills while maintaining user interest through interactive digital interfaces.
*   **Personalized Interventions:** Customized therapy programs tailored to individual needs and progress levels, reinforcing ABA therapy objectives.
*   **Immediate Feedback System:** Real-time feedback mechanisms that help reinforce positive behaviors and correct approaches during therapy sessions.
*   **Progress Tracking:** Comprehensive tracking tools to monitor behavioral improvements, skill acquisition, and therapy effectiveness over time.
*   **Behavioral Data Analysis:** Advanced analytics to identify patterns, measure outcomes, and adjust intervention strategies based on collected data.
*   **Multi-User Support:** Platform designed to facilitate collaboration between therapists, caregivers, and family members in the treatment process.
*   **Evidence-Based Approach:** Built on proven ABA therapy methodologies, incorporating best practices from behavioral analysis research.
*   **Mobile-First Design:** Optimized for mobile devices to take advantage of the strong interest individuals with autism often have in digital technology.
*   **Communication Tools:** Integrated features supporting augmentative and alternative communication (AAC) techniques.
*   **Practice Opportunities:** Structured practice sessions that provide consistent opportunities for skill development and behavior reinforcement.
