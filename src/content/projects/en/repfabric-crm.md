# RepFabric CRM

RepFabric is an AI-driven CRM, sales reporting, and commission tracking platform uniquely tailored to the needs of manufacturers, sales representatives, and distributors. Built by reps for reps, the system streamlines sales management and commission tracking workflows.

**Key Features:**
*   **Comprehensive Sales Tracking:** Track quotes, purchase orders, invoices, and payments including commission calculations in one unified platform.
*   **Opportunity Management:** Advanced opportunities and follow-ups that can be sorted by manufacturing line and customer, supporting multiple lines per opportunity.
*   **AI-Driven Processing:** AI-powered sales data processing with integrated email and calendar syncing for comprehensive sales pipeline tracking.
*   **Email Integration:** Create and update opportunities directly within Outlook or Gmail, with automatic contact syncing to CRM without duplicates.
*   **Mobile-First Approach:** Document sales calls in 60 seconds using voice-to-text technology, capture receipts and expenses in under 30 seconds.
*   **CRMSyncÂ® Technology:** Seamlessly links manufacturer CRMs (Salesforce, MS Dynamics, SugarCRM, etc.) with rep firms' RepFabric system through API access.
*   **Multi-Platform Integration:** Integrates with Microsoft Outlook, Zoho CRM, Gmail, Dynamics 365, Epicor Eclipse, Salesforce Sales Cloud, HubSpot CRM, and Mailchimp.
*   **Enterprise Security:** HTTPS encryption, annual penetration testing, and strict data ownership policies ensure secure cloud-based operations.
*   **Commission Management:** Automated commission tracking and reporting for transparent compensation management.
*   **Sales Reporting:** Comprehensive analytics and reporting tools for data-driven decision making.
