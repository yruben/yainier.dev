# FirstDue

FirstDue is the fastest-growing comprehensive software solution for Fire & EMS departments. Born in the cloud and designed to manage entire fire and emergency medical service operations in one unified platform, accessible from any device.

**Key Features:**
*   **All-in-One Platform:** Consolidates NFIRS, ePCR, fire prevention, pre-incident planning, scheduling, personnel management, asset & inventory, hydrants, training, and community engagement in a single application.
*   **Incident Reporting & NFIRS:** Advanced incident documentation with compliance-focused field tools, automation, and comprehensive analytics for regulatory compliance.
*   **ePCR (Electronic Patient Care Reporting):** NEMSIS v3.4 compliant ePCR providing essential functionality without sacrificing response efficiency for emergency medical services.
*   **Pre-Incident Planning:** Comprehensive property information and highly configurable alert tiles giving crews critical data exactly when needed for safe and intelligent decision-making.
*   **Response & Command:** Digital command board enabling Incident Commanders to swiftly allocate and track resources during critical incidents.
*   **Scheduling & Personnel:** Advanced scheduling system with shift bidding, time-off requests, shift trades, and automated staffing management.
*   **Data & Analytics:** Transform raw operational data into visual, actionable insights empowering leadership to evaluate performance, ensure compliance, and drive better outcomes.
*   **Training & LMS:** Comprehensive training library automatically linked to compliance standards, covering ALS, BLS, Paramedic, Fire Fighter I & II, and industrial firefighting.
*   **AI-Powered Documentation:** Experience AI-powered documentation for faster, more accurate reports with less manual entry, boosting efficiency across all operations.
*   **Cloud-Based Architecture:** Easily implemented, highly configurable, accessible from any device, with consistent platform updates ensuring future-ready capabilities.
