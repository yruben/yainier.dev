# ENZONA

ENZONA is Cuba's comprehensive digital payment platform developed by Xetid (Defense Information Technology Company) in July 2019. The platform revolutionizes electronic commerce and e-government services across the country, providing secure financial transactions for millions of users.

**Key Features:**
*   **Multi-Currency Support:** Handles transactions in Cuban pesos (CUP) and Freely Convertible Currency (USD) from major Cuban banks including Metropolitano, BANDEC, and BPA.
*   **Comprehensive Payment Services:** Money transfers, account balance inquiries, utility payments (water, telephone, electricity), and online payments across multiple entities.
*   **QR Code Payments:** Enables quick and secure contactless payments at physical locations through QR code scanning technology.
*   **E-Commerce Platform:** Includes "Bulevar" feature providing virtual store creation, hosting, security, and operation for digital businesses.
*   **Enterprise Architecture:** Built with Laravel and Java backend, Enterprise Service Bus for integration, and React for modern, responsive frontend experiences.
*   **Government Integration:** Seamlessly integrated with e-government services for streamlined public service payments.
*   **Wide Acceptance Network:** Accepted at bodegas, CUPET gas stations, stores, restaurants, and virtual shops across Cuba.
*   **Mobile-First Design:** Available as a mobile app through enzona.net and APKLIS with free data access provided by ETECSA.
